As a non-English speaker myself, I sometimes find texting in a different format to be super hard, especially when you're an international student. All you learn is formal English, you don't get to know Gen Z words or slang.

Even though you can do it with ChatGPT or any AI model, I'm just getting too lazy to provide the same instructions every time, especially for the same things like "correct my grammar" or "make this message more Gen Z, like texting a close friend."

That's why I decided to build this app. You paste a message you're about to send, a text, DM, email, whatever, pick the vibe you want (casual, formal, friendlier, more direct, Gen Z, you name it), and get a rewritten version back in seconds. One button, that's it. Oh, and it supports 20+ languages too, so if you're learning a new language or just need to write in one, it's got you covered.

I've been using it to rewrite emails and messages to send to my friends for a while now. At first, I gatekept it only for myself, lol, but in one of my uni classes, my friend saw me using it to reply to a group chat, asked me about it, and wanted me to share it with him.
